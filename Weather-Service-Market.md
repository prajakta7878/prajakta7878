Weather Service Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2029
Meteorological services are mainly aimed at meteorological services, which can be divided into decision-making meteorological services, public meteorological services, professional meteorological services and scientific and technological services. Meteorological services belong to the category of public services according to their attributes. Decision-making meteorological services are meteorological services provided by governments at all levels and relevant departments for decision-making. Public meteorological services are daily meteorological services provided to the public; Professional meteorological services are the meteorological services provided for all walks of life according to the needs of the industry; Technical services are meteorological services with special needs provided to specialized users. These four constitute a comprehensive meteorological service network covering the whole society.
