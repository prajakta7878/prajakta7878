Lawn and Garden Supplies Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2029

Lawn and garden supplies mainly include fertilizers, growth medias, grass seeds, pesticides and other products. As people's demand for the environment becomes higher and higher, the demand for gardens and lawns becomes higher and higher, so lawn planting is also very extensive. These products are mainly used to supplement flowers and plants with enough nutrients to promote lawn growth, to keep out pests.
